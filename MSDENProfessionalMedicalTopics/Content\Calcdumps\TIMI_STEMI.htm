Thrombolysis in Myocardial Infarction (TIMI) Score for ST Elevation Acute Myocardial Infarction

 

 DM, history of HTN or history of Angina (1 point)

 Systolic blood pressure less than 100 mm Hg (3 points)

 Heart rate greater than 100 BPM (2 points)

 Killip class II-IV (2 points)

 Body weight <150 lb or 67 kg (1 point)

 Anterior lead ST elevation or left BBB (1 point)

 Time to treat more >4 hours (1 point)
Age

≥75 years old (3 points)

 65-74 years old (2 points)

 <65 years old (0 points)

 
Total Criteria Point Count:
 

 

 TIMI Risk Score Predicts 30 Day Mortality After an MI

 
0 Points: 0.8%
1 Point: 1.6%
2 Points: 2.2%
3 Points: 4.4%
4 Points: 7.3%
5 Points: 12%
6 Points: 16%
7 Points: 23%
8 Points: 27%
9 to 14 Points: 36%

 
Notes
  • Entry criteria: Chest pain for more than 30 minutes, ST elevation, symptom onset less than 6 hours, fibrinolytic eligible.
