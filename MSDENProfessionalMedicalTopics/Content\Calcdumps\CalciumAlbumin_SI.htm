Calcium Correction in Hypoalbuminemia (SI units)

 
Input
 
Serum Ca  
Normal Albumin 40 g/L

 44 g/L
Patient Albumin  

 
Result
 
 
Ca  
 
Decimal Precision  
 

 
Notes
  • The Normal Albumin parameter is set to 40 gm/L.
  • Some practitioners prefer 44 gm/L as normal (see article 1 below.) Simply change the Normal Albumin value above and the page will recalculate instantly.

 
Equations used
 
Ca = SerumCa + 0.02 * (NormalAlbumin - PatientAlbumin)
