Pancreatitis Prognosis Criteria

 
Criteria at time of patient admission to hospital:

 Age >55 (1 point)

 Glucose >200 mg/dL (1 point)

 WBC >16,000 (1 point)

 AST >250 (1 point)

 LDH >350 (1 point)
Criteria that may develop over the first 2 hospital days:

 BUN rises more than 5 mg/dL (1 point)

 Base deficit >4 mEq/L (1 point)

 Hct drops 10% or greater (1 point)

 PO2 <60 mmHg (1 point)

 Calcium <8 mg/dL (1 point)

 Fluid sequestration >6 L (1 point)

 
Total Criteria Point Count:
 

 

 Ranson Criteria

 
0 to 2 points: Mortality is 1%
3 to 4 points: Mortality is 16%
5 to 6 points: Mortality is 40%
7 to 11 points: Mortality almost 100%
