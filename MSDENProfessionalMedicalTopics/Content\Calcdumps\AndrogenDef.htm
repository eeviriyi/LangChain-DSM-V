Androgen Deficiency in Aging Males (ADAM) Screener

 

Consider Testing for Possible Androgen Deficiency: 1 Major Criteria and 0 Minor Criteria

Consider Testing for Possible Androgen Deficiency: 0 Major Criteria and 3 Minor Criteria
 

 
Major Criteria
      Decreased libido
      Decrease in erection strength

 
Minor Criteria
      Decrease in strength, endurance or both
      Loss of body height
      Decreased enjoyment of life
      Sadness, grumpiness, or both
      Recent deterioration in the ability to play sports
      Tendency to fall asleep after dinner
      Recent deterioration in work performance
