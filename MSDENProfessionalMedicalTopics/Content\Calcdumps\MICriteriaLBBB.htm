M.I. Criteria for Likelyhood in Chest Pain with LBBB

 

 ST Elevation ≥1 mm concordant with QRS axis (5 points)

 ST depression ≥1 mm in V1, V2 or V3 (3 points)

 ST Elevation ≥5 mm discordant with QRS axis (2 points)

 M.I. Criteria

 
10 Points: 100 % MI
8 Points: 92 % MI
7 Points: 93 % MI
5 Points: 88 % MI
3 Points: 66 % MI
2 Points: 50 % MI
0 Points: 16 % MI
