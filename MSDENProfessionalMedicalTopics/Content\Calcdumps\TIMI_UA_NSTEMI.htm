Thrombolysis in Myocardial Infarction (TIMI) Score for Unstable Angina Non ST Elevation Myocardial Infarction

 

 Age ≥65 (1 point)

 3 or more CAD risk factors (1 point)

 Known CAD with more than 50% stenosis (1 point)

 Aspirin use in the past 7 days (1 point)

 Severe angina in the preceeding 24 hours (1 point)

 Elevated cardiac markers (1 point)

 ST deviation greater than 0.5 mm (1 point)

 
Total Criteria Point Count:
 

 

 TIMI Score and 2 week Death / MI Risk ** Death / MI / Urgent Revasc Risk Interpretation

 
0 to 1 Point: 3% ** 5%
2 Points: 3% ** 8%
3 Points: 5% ** 13%
4 Points: 7% ** 20%
5 Points: 12% ** 26%
6 to 7 Points: 19% ** 41%

 
Notes
  • Entry criteria:UA or NSTEMII defined as ischemic pain at rest within past 24H, with evidence of CAD (ST segment deviation or +marker).
  • CAD risk factors included hypertension, family history of CAD, elevated cholesterol, diabetes, and smoking.
