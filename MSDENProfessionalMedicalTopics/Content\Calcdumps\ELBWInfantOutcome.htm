 Risk of Poor Outcome

 
0 points: 18% Risk

Notes
  • ELBW stands for Extremely Low Birth Weight.
  • Poor outcome is defined by one of the following occuring by 18 months: 1) Cerebral palsy, 2) Cognitive delay, 3) Severe hearing loss, 4) Bilateral blindness, or 5) Death.
