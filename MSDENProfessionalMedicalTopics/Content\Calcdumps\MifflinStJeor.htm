Mifflin-St. Jeor Resting Energy Expenditure (REE)

 
Input
 
Sex Female (-161)

 Male (5)
Age  
Weight  
Height  

 
Result
 
 
REE  
 
Decimal Precision  
 

 

 
Equations used
 
REE = (10 * Weight) + (6.25 * Height) - (5 * Age) + Sex

 

 
Calculation Details and Variables
  • Equation parameters such as Sex, have two or more discrete values that may be used in the calculation. The numbers in the parentheses, e.g. (-161), represent the values that will be used.

 
References
  1. Mifflin MD, St Jeor ST, Hill LA, et al. A new predictive equation for resting energy expenditure in healthy individuals. Am J Clin Nutr. 1990 Feb;51(2):241-7. PubMed ID: 2305711
