Notes
  • Novel Factor represents the triglyceride to VLDL-cholesterol ratio. This ratio varies depending on the Triglycerides and Non-HDL Cholesterol levels.
  • The Novel Factor improves accuracy of LDL Cholesterol determination throughout a wide range of triglyceride levels.

 
Equations used
 
Non-HDL Cholesterol = Total Cholesterol - HDL Cholesterol
LDL Cholesterol = Non-HDL Cholesterol - (Triglycerides / Novel Factor)
