Decimal Precision  
 

 
Notes
  • In this calculator, mcg is the abbreviation for micrograms.
  • A fractional excretion of amylase greater than 5% may mean acute pancreatitis.
  • Excretion less than 1% may mean macroamylasemia

 
Equations used
 
FEAmylase = 100 * (UrineAmylase * PlasmaCreat) / (PlasmaAmylase * UrineCreat)
