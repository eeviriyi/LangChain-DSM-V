Hepatitis Discriminant Function for Corticosteroid Therapy in Alcoholic Hepatitis

 
Input
 
Prothrombin Time  
Control Prothrombin Time  
Bilirubin  

 
Result
 
 
Index  
 
 

 
Notes
  • Patients with a score ≥32 may benefit from treatment with corticosteroids.

 
Equations used
 
Index = 4.6 * (ProthrombinTime - ControlProthrombinTime) + Bilirubin
