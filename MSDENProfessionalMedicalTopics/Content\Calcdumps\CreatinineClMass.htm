Creatinine Clearance by Mass

 
CreatClear = UrineCreatMass24Hours / SerumCreatConc / 0.0144

 
Input:
 
Urine Creat Mass 24 Hours  
Serum Creat Conc  

 
Result:
 
Creat Clear  
 
Decimal Precision  
 
Notes
  • In this calculator, mcg is the abbreviation for micrograms.
  • This equation is a simple re-write of the standard formula: Clearance = UV/P
  • The volume term V is actually best represented as V dot (for flow), with units of mL/min.
  • The term UV is the mass of creatinine excreted in one day.
  • The constant 0.0144 is a properly scaled version of the number of minutes in one day: 1440
