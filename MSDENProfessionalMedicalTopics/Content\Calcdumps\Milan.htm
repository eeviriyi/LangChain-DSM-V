Milan Criteria in Liver Transplantation for Hepatocellular Carcinoma

 
Number and size of hepatocellular carcinoma tumors in the liver

One tumor ≤5 cm in diameter (0 points)

 Up to three tumors, each ≤3 cm in diameter (0 points)

 More than three tumors (1 point)

 Any tumor >5 cm in diameter (1 point)
Tumor invasion of lymph nodes

No (0 points)

 Yes (1 point)
Tumor invasion of blood vessels

No (0 points)

 Yes (1 point)

 
Total Criteria Point Count:
 

 

 Milan Criteria Interpretation

 
0 points: Transplantation may be considered
1 to 3 points: Transplantation may be futile
