Notes
  • Be sure to go through all of the pull-down choices. Some select more than one linked risk factors (see below.)
  • The Num Biops term refers to the number of prior breast biopsies performed. It is linked to the Age at Counseling.
  • The Age FLB Num Rels term refers to the subject's age when she had her first live birth. It is linked to the number of her relatives that have been diagnosed with breast cancer.

 
Equations used
 
RelRisk = Menarche * NumBiops * AgeFLBNumRels * AtypHyper
FiveYearRisk = RelRisk * BaselineRiskForAge

 

 
Calculation Details and Variables
  • Equation parameters such as Menarche, have two or more discrete values that may be used in the calculation. The numbers in the parentheses, e.g. (1.00), represent the values that will be used.
