Equations used
 
GFR = 169.98 * (SerumCreat/88.4)-0.999 * Age-0.176 * Sex * Race * (BUN/0.357)-0.170 * (Albumin/10)0.318

 

 
Calculation Details and Variables
  • Equation parameters such as Sex, have two or more discrete values that may be used in the calculation. The numbers in the parentheses, e.g. (0.762), represent the values that will be used.
