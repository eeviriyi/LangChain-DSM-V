Pancreatitis Prognosis Criteria When Disease Due To Gallstones

 
Criteria at time of patient admission to hospital:

 Age >70 (1 point)

 Glucose >220 md/dL (1 point)

 WBC >18,000 (1 point)

 AST >250 (1 point)

 LDH >400 (1 point)
Criteria that may develop over the first 2 hospital days:

 BUN rises more than 2 mg/dL (1 point)

 Base deficit >5 mEq/L (1 point)

 Hct drops 10% or greater (1 point)

 Calcium <8 mg/dL (1 point)

 Fluid sequestration >4 L (1 point)

 
Total Criteria Point Count:
 

 

 Pancreatitis Criteria

 
0 to 2 points: Mortality is 1.8%
3 to 4 points: Mortality is 11%
5 to 6 points: Mortality is 33%
7 to 10 points: Mortality almost 100%
