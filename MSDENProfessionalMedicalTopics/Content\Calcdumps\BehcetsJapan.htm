Behcet's Syndrome Japanese Ministry of Health and Welfare Criteria

 

Diagnostic of Incomplete Form : 1 Major Occular Criteria and 1 Other Major Criteria and 0 Minor Criteria

Also Incomplete Form : 1 Major Occular Criteria and 0 Other Major Criteria and 2 Minor Criteria

Diagnostic of Complete Form : 1 Major Occular Criteria and 3 Other Major Criteria and 0 Minor Criteria
 
Major Occular Criteria
      Iridocyclitis often with hypopyon or chorioretinitis
Other Major Criteria
      Recurrent oral aphthous ulcerations
      Genital ulcers
      Skin lesions: erythema nodosum-like lesions, folliculitis, acneform nodules, thrombophlebitis, cutaneous hypersensitivity
Minor Criteria
      Arthritis
      Epididymitis
      Intestinal symptoms attributed to ileocecal ulcers
      Symptomatic nervous system lesions
      Vascular symptoms: obliterative thrombophlebitis, arterial occlusion, aneurysm
