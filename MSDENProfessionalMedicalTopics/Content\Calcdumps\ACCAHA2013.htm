Notes
  • This calculator helps predict the 10-year risk of the following hard ASCVD events:
    • First occurence of nonfatal myocardial infarction
    • CHD death
    • Fatal or nonfatal stroke
  • This calculator may overestimate risk (see Lancet reference) and a discussion with the patient needs to ensue if there are any questions.
  • Risk estimates were developed from cohorts primarily comprising White and African American subjects. Risk may be underestimated in American Indians, Asian Americans of South Asian ancestry, and Puerto Ricans. Risk may be overestimated in Asian Americans of East Asian ancestry and some Mexican Americans.
