Friedewald Equation for Low Density Lipoprotein (LDL-C, SI units)

 
Input
 
Total Chol  
HDL  
Triglyceride  

 
Result
 
 
LDL  
 
Decimal Precision  
 

 
Notes
  • In the formula above LDL represents LDL-C and HDL represents HDL-C.
  • This calculation is not valid when the Triglyceride level is ≥4.5 mmol/L.

 
Equations used
 
LDL = TotalChol - (Triglyceride / 2.2) - HDL

 

 
References
  1. Friedewald WT, Levy RI, Fredrickson DS. Estimation of the concentration of low-density lipoprotein cholesterol in plasma, without use of the preparative ultracentrifuge. Clin Chem. 1972, 18;499-502. PubMed ID: 4337382
