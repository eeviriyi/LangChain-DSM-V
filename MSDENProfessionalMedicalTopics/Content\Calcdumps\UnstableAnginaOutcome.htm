Unstable Angina Outcome Prediction

 

 Patient more than 65 years old (1 point)

 Patient has had prior CABG (1 point)

 Patient was already on aspirin (1 point)

 Patient was already on beta blocker (1 point)

 EKG with ST depression (1 point)

 Risk of Poor Outcome

 
0 to 1 point: 6.5% Risk
2 points: 14.6% Risk
3 points: 22.7% Risk
4 to 5 points: 37.1% Risk

 
Notes
  • Poor outcome is defined by one of the following occuring within 7 days of the initial presentation: 1)Refractory ischemia 2) Myocardial infarction and 3) Death.
