Notes
  • In this calculator, the subject's blood pressure percentiles are based on age and height percentile.
  • Stage I hypertension is defined as either Systolic BP or Diastolic BP between the 95th percentile and the 99th percentile plus 5 mmHg.
  • Stage II hypertension is defined as either Systolic BP or Diastolic BP greater than the 99th percentile plus 5 mmHg.
  • Children and adolescents whose BP exceeds 120/80 mmHg are also considered prehypertensive.
  • At the extremes (>97th percentile or <3rd percentile), small differences in percentiles represent clinically important differences. At these extremes, the Z-score is a more precise reflection of how far the measurement deviates from the mean and is a useful tool for tracking changes.
  • LMS data used in this calculator are shown in the data table below.
  • For all LMS tables, the row in the table corresponding to the patient-specific value is found in the first column of the table; values to be used for L (the Box-Cox power), M (the median), and S (the generalized coefficient of variation) in the equation for Z-score are retrieved.
  • Z-scores may be used to do a table lookup for the corresponding percentile in the Normal table percentiles for Z-score table (below). Numbers along the left side (Y-axis) of the table represent the first number and first decimal place of the Z-score, while the numbers along the top (X-axis) of the table represent the second decimal position of the Z-score.

 
Equations used
 
AgeMonths = Age * 12
HeightZ = ((Height/M)L - 1) / (L * S)
HeightPercentile = ZtoPercentile(HeightZ)
AgeFactor = Age - 10
SystolicBPAvg = 102.19768 + (1.82416 * AgeFactor) + (0.12776 * AgeFactor2) + (0.00249 * AgeFactor3) - (0.00135 * AgeFactor4) + (2.73157 * HeightZ) - (0.19618 * HeightZ2) - (0.04659 * HeightZ3) + (0.00947 * HeightZ4)
DiastolicBPAvg = 61.01217 + (0.68314 * AgeFactor) - (0.09835 * AgeFactor2) + (0.01711 * AgeFactor3) + (0.00045 * AgeFactor4) + (1.46993 * HeightZ) - (0.07849 * HeightZ2) - (0.03144 * HeightZ3) + (0.00967 * HeightZ4)
SystolicBPZ = (SystolicBP - SystolicBPAvg) / 10.7128
DiastolicBPZ = (DiastolicBP - DiastolicBPAvg) / 11.6032
SystolicBPPercentile = ZtoPercentile(SystolicBPZ)
DiastolicBPPercentile = ZtoPercentile(DiastolicBPZ)
SBPThreshold = (2.326348 * 10.7128) + SystolicBPAvg + 5
DBPThreshold = (2.326348 * 11.6032) + DiastolicBPAvg + 5
