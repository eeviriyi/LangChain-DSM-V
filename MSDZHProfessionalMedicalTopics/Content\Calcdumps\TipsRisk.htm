TIPS Risk predictor / Survival predictor (Transjugular Intrahepatic Portosystemic Shunt)

 
RiskScore = (0.957 * ln(Creat)) + (0.378 * ln(Bili)) + (1.12 * ln(INR)) + (0.643 * Cause)
Survival = Dayse(RiskScore-1.127)
